Come To Gombe

Gombe state is one of the most beautiful and peaceful states in northeastern Nigeria.The state was formed on 1 October 1996.
The state has 11 local goverments, and is among the multi lingual states in Nigeria, and of the 36 states of Nigeria, Gombe is the 21st largest in area.
Gombe state is also the 32nd most populous state in Nigeria with an estimated population of about 3.96 million as of 2022. Its motto is "The Jewel of the Savannah".

Among the many places and scenes to visit in Gombe, below are some that one cannot afford to miss:

  1. Dadin Kowa Dam

    2. The Dadin Kowa Dam is situated in Yamaltu Deba local government area of Gombe State.
    It is located in Y/Deba LGA, about 35 kilometers to the east of Gombe city, and provides drinking water for the town.
    The dam was completed by the federal government in 1984, with the goal of providing irrigation and electricity.

  2. Gombe Lawanti International Airport

    3. Gombe Lawanti International Airport also known as Sani Abacha International Airport is located at Lawanti area of Gombe State,
    It was contructed by the Federal Government of Nigeria in 2005. In 2008, the airport was opened for commercial use.

  3. Tangale Hill

    4. This is an enchanting and beautiful volcanic hill south of Gombe. It is one of the highest peaks in Nigeria and it is conical in shape.
    It is located in Shongom LGA in Gombe, 45 minutes' journey from Gombe city. It is considered to be perfect for hikers.

  4. Emirs Palace

    5. The palace was built by the 10th Emir of Gombe, Alhaji Shehu Usman Abubakar. And it is a blend of architectural designs with a touch of royalty.
    The palace also serves as a venue for annual durbars.

  5. Gombe State University

    6. Gombe State University is located in Tudun Wada, an area in Shamaki ward, Gombe State.
    The university boasts of many state of the art infrastructures that are suitable for modern teaching and learning environments.
    The university isbeautifully designed compete with most of the IVY league Universities of the world.
    The University currently runs about 40 Programmes under numerous Faculties and Departments.
